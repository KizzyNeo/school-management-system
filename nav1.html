    • Quaker Education

    ABC is a Quaker school. Quakerism holds that there is an inner light in each of us. This belief yields a deep commitment to equality and community, and people of all faiths find resonance in the core human values at the center of Friends (Quaker) education and practice.

    At ABC, we create an academic culture of deep reflection, expansive curiosity, and spirited inquiry by applying Quaker values every day.

    Learning in community

    Learning at ABC is a shared enterprise. We ask and listen respectfully, grant grace in the process of learning and delight in the growth of others.

    Ambition for Greater Good

    Every day, our students become people who are ambitiios not only for themselves, but also for their neighbours and those they have yet to meet.
